tikki-chat!

lets learn to make spicy sweet and tangy aloo chole tikki chat

Ingredients -

  • Aloo tikki
  • Punjabi chole
  • chutneys
  • dahi
  • bhujiya
  • ps. in the end sprinkle some chaat masala and finely chopped coriander leaves.

Directions -

  1. Boil the potatoes
  2. Mash the boiled potatoes
  3. add peas in a bowl
  4. Mash both together
  5. Add finely chopped green chilies, cilantro, red chili powder, chaat masala and salt.
  6. Squeeze fresh lemon juice. Mix everything together.
  7. Divide the mixture into 6 equal portions and make patty out of it.
  8. Heat the skillet on medium heat for shallow frying.
  9. Add few teaspoons of oil. Once oil is medium hot, put the tikkis gently and carefully on the pan.
  10. Drizzle some oil on tikki as well. Cook till bottom side is brown and crispy.
  11. Once cooked, flip the tikkis using spatula and let it brown other side as well.
  12. Remove it to a plate and keep it aside.
  13. Warm up the leftover chole in microwave.
  14. Also prep other elements for making chaat like onion, tomato, yogurt and both chutneys.
  15. Arrange 3 aloo tikki in a plate
  16. Pour Â½ cup of chole on top and sides.
  17. drizzle 1 to 2 tablespoons of green chutney or mint chutney.
  18. drizzle 1 to 2 tablespoons of tamarind date chutney.
  19. Then drizzle 1-2 tablespoons of yogurt.
  20. Sprinkle chopped onions and tomatoes.
  21. Lastly garnish with 2-3 tablespoons of bhujiya.
